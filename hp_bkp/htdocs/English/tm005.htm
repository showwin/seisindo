"Mibyou wo Naosu" Forecasting the trouble that may develop in the future and prevent it from hapenning beforehand.
　TCM considers that the trouble develops because of loss of the balance of the body.

　However, there is homeostasis in living creatures. Therefore, there is a power trying to resume to the previoud condition even when the balance is lost a little. This is so called "Natural healing power. " When comparing healthy status with the "Stick" that stands squarely from ground, when you are sick this stick is inclined by 60 degrees or more. The symptom of the trouble does not appear even if this stick is inclined at 70 or 80 degrees. Moreover, a little inclining can be resumed to previous position by Natural healing power. However, various signs are given as this inclination grows, and it approaches 60 degrees. For instance, these appear that dewatering of skin, sneezing, coldness of limbs and flush. However, you will not think you will be sick for sure. To maintain "Natural healing power" which a person and animals have, we have to be in accordance with nature, not go against it. The animal that lives in the forest and the jungle will be able to do so. However, it is difficult for a man who lives in the civilized society to do it. He lives in the space where it is enclosed by concrete, air conditioned and he might have to work very late at night and/or very early in the morning. With this, "Natural healing power" cannot be maintained. Then, power to resume inclined stick to a straight position disappeards. The sickness develops when inclining little by little and became 60 degrees. Many people as well may say that "Supplement is taken for such time just in case".
